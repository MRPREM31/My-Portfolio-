My Journey

I was born on 31st March 2006, and my learning journey began at the age of 3 at Saraswati Sishu Mandir. It was a wonderful place that gave me a strong foundation — both in studies and in values. The school’s culture of daily prayers and discipline helped shape my personality and thinking. I studied there up to Class 5, building the pillars of my future.

After that, I joined Odisha Adarsha Vidyalaya (OAV), which marked a turning point in my life. This is where English-medium education started, and the learning environment was excellent. I made great friends and was guided by amazing teachers who helped me grow stronger, both academically and personally. I completed my Class 10 here — a milestone I’ll always be proud of.

Then came my higher secondary journey at Nalanda Shree Higher Secondary School, Berhampur. Though it was just a short span of 2 years, I found some of my best friends here — supportive, understanding, and lifelong. My journey through Class 11 and 12 was full of learning and preparation for what came next.

Now, I’m proud to be at NIST University, Berhampur, where my engineering journey has begun. This phase marks the beginning of my dreams — exploring technology, building skills, and aiming high toward becoming something great in life.
